Why Isolation Forest?
The Problem:
Security attacks are rare and constantly evolving. We can't train on "known attacks" because new attacks appear daily.
The Solution:
Isolation Forest learns what "normal" looks like and flags anything that doesn't fit â€” without needing labeled attack data.
How It Works (Simple Explanation):
Imagine you're playing "20 questions" to identify a specific data point:

Normal points are clustered together â†’ need MANY questions to isolate
Anomalies are far from others â†’ need FEW questions to isolate

The algorithm builds random decision trees and measures how quickly each point gets isolated. Points that isolate quickly = anomalies.
